📚 What is EXIF Metadata?

What It Contains

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) stores camera settings, timestamps, GPS location, and other technical data in your photos.

Privacy Concerns

Photos shared online can leak your location, device info, and when/where the photo was taken. Stripping metadata protects your privacy.

When to Keep It

Keep metadata for archival purposes, photo organization, or professional portfolios. Remove it before sharing publicly.
